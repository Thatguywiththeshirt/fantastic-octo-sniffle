Roboso Medical Group: AI Workloads & Responsible AI

Interactive scenario game • Estimated time: 10–15 minutes

Scenario: Roboso Medical Group (CMG)

Roboso Medical Group runs clinical drug trials and wants to use AI in a way that is HIPAA-compliant, cost-conscious, and trustworthy for patients and staff.

They collect patient symptom data through an app, capture images of skin conditions, maintain large collections of paper records, and have overwhelmed support staff who provide inconsistent answers across phone, web, and mobile app.

Before You Play: Key Concepts

Key AI Workloads

  • Conversational AI / Virtual Agents: Chatbots that handle questions via chat, voice, or app.
  • Computer Vision: AI that can interpret and analyze images (for example, skin condition photos).
  • Document Intelligence / OCR: AI that can digitize and extract information from scanned or handwritten documents.

Responsible AI Principles

  • Transparency: Be clear about how and why the AI makes decisions.
  • Privacy & Security: Protect patient data, especially PHI under HIPAA.
  • Fairness: Ensure no patient group is systematically disadvantaged.
  • Reliability & Safety: Test AI thoroughly so it works as intended, safely.

Your Mission: AI Triage at CMG

You are the AI lead for Roboso Medical Group. Your job is to match the right AI workload and Responsible AI principle to each problem.

AI Workload Triage

Question 1
Difficulty: Easy

CMG’s customer support team is overwhelmed by calls and messages from trial participants, and answers differ across phone, web, and app. Which AI workload should CMG use to support the customer support department?

Conversational AI / chatbots / virtual agents
✅ Correct! A virtual agent can handle common questions consistently across phone, web, and in‑app chat, freeing human agents for complex cases.
Computer vision
❌ Not the best fit. Computer vision focuses on interpreting images, not conversations across channels.
Document intelligence / OCR
❌ Close, but not ideal. This workload is better for digitizing paper forms and prescriptions, not live support interactions.

Hint: Think “chat, call, and Q&A” all in one place.

Question 2
Difficulty: Easy

CMG now collects a high volume of patient skin images through their mobile app to track reactions to new drugs. Staff cannot manually review all of them. Which AI workload should analyze these images?

Conversational AI / chatbots / virtual agents
❌ Nope. Conversational AI is designed for text or voice-based dialog, not direct image interpretation.
Computer vision
✅ Correct! Computer vision can classify and analyze medical images at scale to help triage which ones need urgent human review.
Document intelligence / OCR
❌ Not this one. Document intelligence is great for text and forms, but not for pixel-level analysis of skin condition photos.

Hint: Which workload literally “looks at” pixels?

Question 3
Difficulty: Medium

CMG has large sets of handwritten and scanned paper medical records, including history and prescriptions, that must be entered into their digital system. Which AI workload is the best fit here?

Conversational AI / chatbots / virtual agents
❌ Not ideal. Conversational AI helps with dialog, not with extracting structured information from scans and forms.
Raw computer vision only
❌ Incomplete. Generic image recognition alone isn’t enough. You need a workload that can recognize characters and fields in documents.
Document intelligence / OCR
✅ Correct! Document intelligence with OCR can digitize and extract data from handwritten and scanned records into structured formats.

Hint: Think “scan, read, and extract text from forms.”

Part 2

Responsible AI Checkpoint

Match each situation to the Responsible AI principle that best addresses it. Click each option to reveal feedback.

Question 4
Difficulty: Easy

CMG wants trial participants to trust the new support bot. Users should understand that they are talking to AI, what data is being used, and what the bot can and cannot do. Which Responsible AI principle is most important here?

Transparency
✅ Correct! Transparency builds trust by clearly explaining that users are interacting with AI, including its capabilities and limitations.
Privacy & Security
❌ Important, but not the focus here. Privacy & Security are essential, but this scenario is about communicating clearly with users, which is transparency.
Fairness
❌ Not the best fit. Fairness matters, but this question is about how the system explains itself and sets expectations for users.

Hint: Think “clear communication and explanation.”

Question 5
Difficulty: Easy

CMG handles highly sensitive patient data (including PHI) in a HIPAA‑regulated environment. Which Responsible AI principle is directly focused on protecting this personal information and managing data securely?

Transparency
❌ Not quite. Transparency is about clarity and explanation, but not specifically about securing the data itself.
Privacy & Security
✅ Correct! Privacy & Security focus on protecting personal data, controlling access, and complying with regulations like HIPAA.
Reliability & Safety
❌ Not the primary concern here. Reliability & Safety ensure the system works safely, but data protection is specifically covered by Privacy & Security.

Hint: Think “protect PHI, encryption, access controls.”

Question 6
Difficulty: Medium

CMG’s clinical trials serve diverse patient populations. CMG wants to ensure that the AI system does not systematically under‑diagnose or under‑serve any group (for example, by age, gender, race, or geography). Which Responsible AI principle is most relevant?

Fairness
✅ Correct! Fairness focuses on ensuring that no group is unfairly disadvantaged or treated unjustly by the AI system.
Transparency
❌ Related, but not the main idea. Transparency helps explain how the AI works, but the concern here is about equal treatment across groups, which is fairness.
Privacy & Security
❌ Also important, but not the focus. Privacy & Security protect data, but the key issue here is outcomes and equal treatment, which is fairness.

Hint: Think “bias, equity, and equal treatment.”
